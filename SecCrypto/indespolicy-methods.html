INDESPolicy Methods

The INDESPolicy interface supports the following methods.

In this section

Topic Description
GenerateChallenge method Performs the policy decision whether to issue a challenge password to the SCEP client.
Initialize method Initializes the NDES policy module.
Notify method Notifies the plug-in of the transaction status of the SCEP certificate request.
Uninitialize method Uninitializes the NDES policy module.
VerifyRequest method Verifies the NDES certificate request for submission to the CA.
