Device Information Properties (WIA_DIP_XXXX)

Device Information Properties are properties that describe the setup and installation of the device. These properties are available through the IWiaDevMgr or IWiaDevMgr2 interfaces and also through the root item. Device information properties are prefixed with WIA_DIP (Device Information Property), and are supplied by WIA. For more information, see Device Information Property Constants.
