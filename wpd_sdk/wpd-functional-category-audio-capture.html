WPD_FUNCTIONAL_CATEGORY_AUDIO_CAPTURE

A WPD_FUNCTIONAL_CATEGORY_AUDIO_CAPTURE functional object encapsulates audio capture functionality on the device (for example, a voice recorder or other audio recording component).
