ICertificateAttestationChallenge Methods

The ICertificateAttestationChallenge interface supports the following methods.

In this section

Topic Description
DecryptChallenge method Decrypts the challenge from the Certificate Management over CMS (CMC) response and creates a re-encrypted response to send to the CA.
Initialize method Initialize using the full Certificate Management over CMS (CMC) response returned from the CA.
