WINBIO_FRAMEWORK_CHANGE Constants

The following constants can be used in the WinBioAsyncMonitorFrameworkChanges function to identify the type of change that occurred in the framework.

WINBIO_FRAMEWORK_CHANGE_UNIT

0x00000001

A biometric unit was attached to or detached from the computer.
