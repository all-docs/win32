WINBIO_BIR_QUALITY Constants

The following flags are used by the DataQuality member of the WINBIO_BIR_HEADER structure to specify the relative quality of biometric data in the BIR if an integer value from 0 to 100 has not been specified.

Constant/value Description
WINBIO_DATA_QUALITY_NOT_SET 1 Quality measurements are supported by the BIR creator, but no value is set in the BIR.
WINBIO_DATA_QUALITY_NOT_SUPPORTED 2 Quality measurements are not supported by the BIR creator.
