WINBIO_ANSI_381_POS Fingerprint Constants

The following constants can be used to specify the fingers scanned by a sensor:

Remarks

The WINBIO_FINGER_UNSPECIFIED_POS_* constants are not part of the ANSI 381 specification. You can use these constants to enroll up to ten fingers (per account SID) that have no associated position information.

[!IMPORTANT]

Do not attempt to validate fingerprint constant values. The Windows Biometrics Service will validate supplied fingerprint constant values before passing them through to your implementation.
