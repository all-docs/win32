About People Near Me

People Near Me (PNM) is a way for people and applications to advertise themselves and their interests for easy connection and collaboration. The "Near" in People Near Me is the set of users that are located on the same subnet. PNM is a new capability of Windows Peer Networking that is included in WindowsÂ Vista.
