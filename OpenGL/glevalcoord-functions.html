glEvalCoord Functions

These functions evaluate enabled one-dimensional or two-dimensional maps:

  • glEvalCoord1d
  • glEvalCoord1f
  • glEvalCoord2d
  • glEvalCoord2f
  • glEvalCoord1dv
  • glEvalCoord2dv
  • glEvalCoord1fv
  • glEvalCoord2fv
