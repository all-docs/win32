Windows8Api.JetOpenTemporaryTable2 method

Creates a temporary table with a single index. A temporary table stores and retrieves records just like an ordinary table created using JetCreateTableColumnIndex. However, temporary tables are much faster than ordinary tables due to their volatile nature. They can also be used to very quickly sort and perform duplicate removal on record sets when accessed in a purely sequential manner. Also see JetOpenTempTable(JET_SESID, [], Int32, TempTableGrbit, JET_TABLEID, []), "Api.JetOpenTempTable2", JetOpenTempTable3(JET_SESID, [], Int32, JET_UNICODEINDEX, TempTableGrbit, JET_TABLEID, []).
