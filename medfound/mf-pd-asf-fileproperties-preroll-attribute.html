MF_PD_ASF_FILEPROPERTIES_PREROLL attribute

Specifies the amount of time, in milliseconds, to buffer data before playing an Advanced Systems Format (ASF) file.

Data type

UINT64

Remarks

This attribute applies to presentation descriptors for ASF content.

The IMFASFContentInfo::GeneratePresentationDescriptor method generates this attribute from the ASF metadata.
