Sensor Profile GUIDs

The following GUIDS define the different types of sensor profiles. These GUIDS are most commonly used in the SENSORPROFILEID structure.

KSCAMERAPROFILE_HighFrameRate

High frame rate.

KSCAMERAPROFILE_HDRWithWCGVideo

HDR with WCG video.

KSCAMERAPROFILE_HDRWithWCGPhoto

HDR with WCG photo.

KSCAMERAPROFILE_VariablePhotoSequence

Variable photo sequence.

KSCAMERAPROFILE_VideoHDR8

Video HDR8.

KSCAMERAPROFILE_CompressedCamera

The profile supports compressed camera streams to enable saving compressed streams directly to a compressed format without incurring the cost of decompressing and then recompressing the stream.
