D3DAUTHENTICATEDCHANNEL_CONFIGUREINITIALIZE structure

Contains input data for a D3DAUTHENTICATEDCONFIGURE_INITIALIZE command.

To send this query, call IDirect3DAuthenticatedChannel9::Configure.
