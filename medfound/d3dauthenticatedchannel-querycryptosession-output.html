D3DAUTHENTICATEDCHANNEL_QUERYCRYPTOSESSION_OUTPUT structure

Contains the response to a D3DAUTHENTICATEDQUERY_CRYPTOSESSION query.

To send this query, call IDirect3DAuthenticatedChannel9::Query.
