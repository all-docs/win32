RemoteGetCurrentMediaType

Remotable version of the IMFMediaTypeHandler::GetCurrentMediaType method.

[call_as(GetCurrentMediaType)]
HRESULT RemoteGetCurrentMediaType(
    BYTE **ppbData,
    DWORD *pcbData
);

Remarks

Applications cannot call this method directly, and objects do not implement this method. The method does not appear in the vtable for the interface. If GetCurrentMediaType is called across process boundaries, the Media Foundation proxy/stub DLL translates the call into a call to the remote method and then translates it back.
