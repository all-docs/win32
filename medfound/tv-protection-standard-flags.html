TV Protection Standard Flags

The constants in the following table specify television standards and formats for Output Protection Manager (OPM).

Constant/value Description
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_OTHER 0x80000000 The protection standard is unknown.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_NONE 0x00000000 No protection standard is in place.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_IEC61880_525I 0x00000001 The protection standard is IEC 61880, 525i.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_IEC61880_2_525I 0x00000002 The protection standard is IEC 61880-2, 525i.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_IEC62375_625P 0x00000004 The protection standard is IEC 62375, 625p.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_EIA608B_525 0x00000008 The protection standard is EIA/CEA-608-B, 525i.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_EN300294_625I 0x00000010 The protection standard is ETSI EN 300 294, 625i.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_CEA805A_TYPEA_525P 0x00000020 The protection standard is CEA-805-A Type A, 525p.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_CEA805A_TYPEA_750P 0x00000040 The protection standard is CEA-805-A Type A, 750p.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_CEA805A_TYPEA_1125I 0x00000080 The protection standard is CEA-805-A Type A, 1125i.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_CEA805A_TYPEB_525P 0x00000100 The protection standard is CEA-805-A Type B, 525p.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_CEA805A_TYPEB_750P 0x00000200 The protection standard is CEA-805-A Type B, 750p.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_CEA805A_TYPEB_1125I 0x00000400 The protection standard is CEA-805-A Type B, 1125i.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_ARIBTRB15_525I 0x00000800 The protection standard is ARIB TR-B15, 525i.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_ARIBTRB15_525P 0x00001000 The protection standard is ARIB TR-B15, 525p.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_ARIBTRB15_750P 0x00002000 The protection standard is ARIB TR-B15, 750p.
OPM_PROTECTION_STANDARD_ARIBTRB15_1125I 0x00004000 The protection standard is ARIB TR-B15, 1125i.

Remarks

These flags are numerically equivalent to the COPP_TVProtectionStandard enumeration used in Certified Output Protection Protocol (COPP).
