APIs Demonstrated

This sample demonstrates the following APIs.

  • IAudioSessionControl
  • IAudioSessionManager
  • IMFPMediaItem
  • IMFPMediaPlayer
  • IMFPMediaPlayerCallback
  • IMMDevice
  • IMMDeviceEnumerator
  • ISimpleAudioVolume
