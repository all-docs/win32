Direct3D 12 Media Foundation GUIDs

This page specifies the GUIDs for the Direct3D 12 GUIDs for Media Foundation.

MF_D3D12_SYNCHRONIZATION_OBJECT

The GUID identifying a Direct3D Media Foundation synchronization object. Pass this value to IMFDXGIBuffer::SetUnknown and IMFDXGIBuffer::GetUnknown to access a Direct3D 12 Media Foundation synchronization object.
