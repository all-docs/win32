IMAGELISTSTATEFLAGS

The following flags are passed to the IImageList::Draw method in the fState member of IMAGELISTDRAWPARAMS.

Constant/value Description
ILS_NORMAL 0x00000000 The image state is not modified.
ILS_GLOW 0x00000001 Not supported.
ILS_SHADOW 0x00000002 Not supported.
ILS_SATURATE 0x00000004 Reduces the color saturation of the icon to grayscale. This only affects 32bpp images.
ILS_ALPHA 0x00000008 Alpha blends the icon. Alpha blending controls the transparency level of an icon, according to the value of its alpha channel. The value of the alpha channel is indicated by the frame member in the IMAGELISTDRAWPARAMS method. The alpha channel can be from 0 to 255, with 0 being completely transparent, and 255 being completely opaque.
