WMI-Specific Qualifiers

In addition to the CIM qualifiers, WMI provides a number of additional qualifiers to further refine the provision of class instances and operations.

The following is a list of additional qualifiers that WMI provides:

  • Standard WMI Qualifiers
  • Qualifiers Specific to the SNMP Provider
  • Qualifiers Specific to the View Provider
  • Qualifiers Specific to the WDM Provider
  • Qualifiers Specific to WMI Performance Classes

In addition to qualifiers, flavors describing the behavior of qualifiers may be applied to the various qualifiers. For more information, see Qualifier Flavors.
