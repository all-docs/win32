/no_warn switch

The /no_warn switch directs the MIDL compiler to suppress warning messages.

midl /no_warn

Switch Options

This switch has no parameters.

Remarks

The use of the /no_warn switch is equivalent to /W0.

Examples

midl /no_warn filename.idl

midl /W0 filename.idl
