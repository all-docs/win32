Mesh Functions (Direct3D 10 Graphics)

D3DX supplies the following mesh functions.

  • D3DX10CreateMesh
  • D3DX10CreateSkinInfo
  • D3DXboxBoundProbe
  • D3DXComputeBoundingBox
  • D3DXComputeBoundingSphere
  • D3DXIntersectTri
  • D3DXSphereBoundProbe
