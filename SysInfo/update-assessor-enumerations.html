Update Assessor Enumerations

The following enumerations are used by the Update Accessor provider.

Function Description
UpdateAssessmentStatus Describes how up-to-date the OS on a device is.
UpdateImpactLevel Indicates a high, medium, or low impact of a device running an out-of-date OS.
