LOCALE_ILDATE

Long date format-ordering specifier. It is preferred for your application to use the LOCALE_SLONGDATE constant instead of LOCALE_ILDATE.

Value Meaning
0 Month-Day-Year
1 Day-Month-Year
2 Year-Month-Day
