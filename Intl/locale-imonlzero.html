LOCALE_IMONLZERO

Specifier for leading zeros in month fields for a short date only. It is preferred for your application to use the LOCALE_SSHORTDATE constant instead of LOCALE_IMONLZERO.

Value Meaning
0 No leading zeros for months
1 Leading zeros for months
