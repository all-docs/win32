LOCALE_ITLZERO

Specifier for leading zeros in time fields. It is preferred for your application to use the LOCALE_STIMEFORMAT constant instead of LOCALE_ITLZERO.

Value Meaning
0 No leading zeros for hours
1 Leading zeros for hours
