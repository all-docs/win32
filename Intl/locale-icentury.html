LOCALE_ICENTURY

A 2-digit or 4-digit century for the short date only. The century can have one of the following values. It is preferred for your application to use LOCALE_SSHORTDATE instead of LOCALE_ICENTURY.

Value Meaning
0 Abbreviated 2-digit century
1 Full 4-digit century
