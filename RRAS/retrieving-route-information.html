Retrieving Route Information

There are three methods used to obtain route information from the routing table manager.

  • Enumerating routes (described in Enumerating Routing Table Entries).
  • Searching for specific routes (described in Finding Specific Information in the Routing Table).
  • Retrieving changed destinations (described in Receiving Notification of Changes).
