Account Object Protection

Account objects are protected in the following fashion:

  • The group WORLD has GENERIC_EXECUTE.
  • The group LOCAL_ADMIN has DELETE, GENERIC_READ, GENERIC_WRITE, GENERIC_EXECUTE, and WRITE_DACL access.
  • The group LOCAL_ADMIN is assigned as owner and primary group of Account objects.
