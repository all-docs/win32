Discharge Policy Constants

The following list describes the discharge policy constants.

Constant/value Description
DISCHARGE_POLICY_CRITICAL 0 Which of the battery discharge policy settings (SYSTEM_POWER_LEVEL structures) is used when the battery discharges below the critical threshold.
DISCHARGE_POLICY_LOW 1 Which of the battery discharge policy settings (SYSTEM_POWER_LEVEL structures) is used when the battery discharges below the low threshold.
NUM_DISCHARGE_POLICIES 4 Number of battery discharge policy settings (SYSTEM_POWER_LEVEL structures) specified.

Requirements

Requirement Value
