RayDesc structure

Passed to the TraceRay function to define the origin, direction, and extents of the ray.

Syntax

struct RayDesc
{
    float3 Origin;
    float  TMin;
    float3 Direction;
    float  TMax;
};

Fields

Origin

The origin of the ray.

TMin

The minimum extent of the ray.

Direction

The direction of the ray.

TMax

The maximum extent of the ray.
