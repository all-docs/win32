Painting and Drawing Messages

The following messages are used with painting and drawing:

  • WM_DISPLAYCHANGE
  • WM_ERASEBKGND
  • WM_NCPAINT
  • WM_PAINT
  • WM_PRINT
  • WM_PRINTCLIENT
  • WM_SETREDRAW
  • WM_SYNCPAINT
