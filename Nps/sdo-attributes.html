Attributes (Network Policy Server)

Use the following enumeration types when manipulating RADIUS dictionary attributes on the SDO computer.

  • ATTRIBUTEFILTER
  • ATTRIBUTEID
  • ATTRIBUTEINFO
  • ATTRIBUTEPROPERTIES
  • ATTRIBUTERESTRICTIONS
  • ATTRIBUTESYNTAX
  • AUTHENTICATION_TYPE
  • CLIENTPROPERTIES
  • CONDITIONPROPERTIES
  • DICTIONARYPROPERTIES
  • EAPWRAPPROPERTIES
  • IASCOMMONPROPERTIES
  • POLICYPROPERTIES
  • PROFILEPROPERTIES
