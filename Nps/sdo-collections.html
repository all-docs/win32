Collections (Network Policy Server)

The values in the following enumeration types correspond to SDO collections.

  • DICTIONARYPROPERTIES
  • IASCOMPONENTPROPERTIES
  • IASPROPERTIES
  • NAPPROPERTIES
  • POLICYPROPERTIES
  • PROFILEPROPERTIES
  • PROTOCOLPROPERTIES
  • RADIUSPROPERTIES
  • RADIUSSERVERGROUPPROPERTIES
  • REMEDIATIONSERVERGROUPPROPERTIES
  • REMEDIATIONSERVERPROPERTIES
  • SHVTEMPLATEPROPERTIES
