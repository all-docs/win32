Manual Terminal Selection

If the rules of Default selection do not meet the needs of the application, the application has the option of selecting the terminal as it always has: that is, it can use ITStream to enumerate the streams present on the call and select the terminal on the appropriate streams (or, in case of multitrack terminals, create tracks for the streams and select created tracks on the streams).
