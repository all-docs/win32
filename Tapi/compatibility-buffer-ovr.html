Compatibility Buffer

The compatibility buffer is specific to the ISDN Q.931 standard. Please refer to your service provider's documentation on the meaning and use of this data.

Not all service providers support use of this information.

TAPI 2.x: See lineGetCallInfo (dwHighLevelCompSize, dwHighLevelCompOffset, dwLowLevelCompSize, and dwLowLevelCompOffset members of lpCallInfo).

TAPI 3.x: See ITCallInfo::get_CallInfoBuffer (CIB_HIGHLEVELCOMPATIBILITYBUFFER and CIB_LOWLEVELCOMPATIBILITYBUFFER member of CALLINFO_BUFFER).
