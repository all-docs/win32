LINEGROUPSTATUS_ Constants

The LINEGROUPSTATUS_ constants specify the ACD group status.

LINEGROUPSTATUS_GROUPREMOVED

This group has been removed from the list of those available.

LINEGROUPSTATUS_NEWGROUP

A group has been added to the list of those available.
