VCOMM

A communications device driver within TAPI. VCOMM can assign protected mode to TAPI services and allows Windows applications and drivers to use ports and modems. See UniModem.

voice signal

Considered to be any signal that can travel over a 3.1 kHz-bandwidth channel.
