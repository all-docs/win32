Call Center Messages

Messages are used to notify the application of asynchronous events. This section contains the reference for the following TAPI call center messages:

  • LINE_AGENTSESSIONSTATUS
  • LINE_AGENTSPECIFIC
  • LINE_AGENTSTATUS
  • LINE_AGENTSTATUSEX
  • LINE_GROUPSTATUS
  • LINE_PROXYREQUEST
  • LINE_PROXYSTATUS
  • LINE_QUEUESTATUS
