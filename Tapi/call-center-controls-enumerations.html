Call Center Controls Enumerations

TAPI 3.x call center controls use the following enumerations to set and report on events and states.

  • ACDGROUP_EVENT
  • ACDQUEUE_EVENT
  • AGENT_EVENT
  • AGENTHANDLER_EVENT
  • AGENT_SESSION_EVENT
  • AGENT_SESSION_STATE
  • AGENT_STATE
