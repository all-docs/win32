LINEQOSREQUESTTYPE_ Constants

These constants are used by a TSP to identify the type of a QoS (Quality of Service) request being made. Currently, only a service level request is supported. See LINEQOSSERVICELEVEL_ Constants.

LINEQOSREQUESTTYPE_SERVICELEVEL

0x00000001

The QoS request being made is for a particular service level.
