LINEAGENTSTATUS_ Constants

The LINEAGENTSTATUS_ constants list the update status of the members of the LINEAGENTSTATUS structure for an agent.

LINEAGENTSTATUS_ACTIVITY

The dwActivityID, dwActivitySize, or dwActivityOffset member in LINEAGENTSTATUS has been updated.

LINEAGENTSTATUS_ACTIVITYLIST

The LINEAGENTACTIVITYLIST has been updated. The application can call lineGetAgentActivityList to get the updated list.

LINEAGENTSTATUS_CAPSCHANGE

The capabilities in LINEAGENTCAPS have been updated. The application can call lineGetAgentCaps to get the updated list.

LINEAGENTSTATUS_GROUP

The LINEAGENTSTATUS has been updated.

LINEAGENTSTATUS_GROUPLIST

The LINEAGENTGROUPLIST has been updated. The application can call lineGetAgentGroupList to get the updated list.

LINEAGENTSTATUS_NEXTSTATE

The dwNextState member in LINEAGENTSTATUS has been updated.

LINEAGENTSTATUS_STATE

The dwState member in LINEAGENTSTATUS has been updated.

LINEAGENTSTATUS_VALIDNEXTSTATES

The dwValidNextStates member in LINEAGENTSTATUS has been updated.

LINEAGENTSTATUS_VALIDSTATES

The dwValidStates member in LINEAGENTSTATUS has been updated.
