What's New for TSPI Version 2.2

TSPI Version 2.2 changes consist primarily of a number of data structures which have additional elements. The majority of these structures are defined in tapi.h, and are used by both TAPI applications and service providers as a means of passing information.

Functions and messages that were new or changed for TSPI version 2.2 are as follows:

  • LINE_AGENTSESSIONSTATUS
  • LINE_QUEUESTATUS
  • LINE_AGENTSTATUSEX
  • LINE_GROUPSTATUS
  • LINE_PROXYSTATUS

LINEADDRCAPFLAGS_ACDGROUP member of LINEADDRCAPFLAGS_ Constants

LINEERR_INVALAGENTSESSIONSTATE and LINEERR_DISCONNECTED members of LINEERR_ Constants.

  • LINEAGENTSTATEEX_ Constants
  • LINEAGENTSTATUSEX_ Constants
  • LINEAGENTSESSIONSTATE_ Constants
  • LINEAGENTSESSIONSTATUS_ Constants
  • LINEQUEUESTATUS_ Constants
  • LINEGROUPSTATUS_ Constants
  • LINEPROXYSTATUS_ Constants
  • LINEPROXYREQUEST_ Constants
