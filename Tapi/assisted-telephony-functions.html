Assisted Telephony Functions

TAPI supports the following Assisted Telephony functions:

  • tapiGetLocationInfo
  • tapiRequestDrop
  • tapiRequestMakeCall
  • tapiRequestMediaCall

For a categorization of TAPI functions by service level and task, see TAPI Quick Function Reference.
