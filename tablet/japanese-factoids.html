Japanese Factoids, part 3

Factoid JapaneseCommon
Description All KanjiCommon, Hiragana, and Katakana characters. In addition, common alphanumeric characters, standard punctuation, and symbols are included.The JapaneseCommon factoid is useful for standard text entry where infrequently used characters are not needed.
