SENSOR_CATEGORY_UNSUPPORTED

The SENSOR_CATEGORY_UNSUPPORTED category contains sensors that fall outside of any other defined category (including SENSOR_CATEGORY_OTHER).

SENSOR_TYPE_UNKNOWN

The sensor device-driver could not determine the category of type of the given sensor.
