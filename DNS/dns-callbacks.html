DNS callbacks

The following callbacks are defined for DNS.

  • DNS_QUERY_RAW_COMPLETION_ROUTINE
  • DNS_SERVICE_BROWSE_CALLBACK
  • DNS_SERVICE_REGISTER_COMPLETE
  • DNS_SERVICE_RESOLVE_COMPLETE
  • MDNS_QUERY_CALLBACK
